About Clever Cat

shop slower, shop smarter

In 2022, two cat people got together and decided they were fed up with the trend cycle. So they got together and created Clever Cat Vintage out of a shared love for fashion and sustainability.

On average, an article of clothing is worn only 7 times before it is discarded. Our goal is to stop contributing to this cycle of fast fashion. We promise to sell clothes that are comfortable, classic, and affordable.

where to find us

Clever Cat Vintage is located at the flea market at 201 S Estes Dr, Chapel Hill NC.

Come visit us on Saturdays from 9am-12pm!
